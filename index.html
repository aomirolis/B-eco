Our Services

For customers

We provide express door to door deliveries for any type of small packages by using our app: B-eco

For small businesses

We cooperate with e-shops or stores that do not work with another courier to deliver their packages.

For transporters

We are looking for people with a bike or any other transportation mean, willing to keep up with the pace of our project and come through any type of challenges.
